WHO IS
RAZMIK

Stand-up comedian based in Warsaw, Poland, performing entirely in English. No safety net. No translator needed.

From Warsaw stages to Budapest clubs, Copenhagen basements, and the Baltic coast — the material is personal, direct, and lands exactly where it's supposed to.

The kind of comedy that makes you laugh first and think second. Or both at once. Either way, you're not leaving indifferent.
